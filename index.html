PhD student under the supervision of Antoine Chambaz, Christophe Denis and Mohamed Hebiri.

I study Algorithmic Fairness for Multiclass Classification and Causal Frameworks.

After a double Bachelor’s program at Université Paris Cité, I went for a Part III (Master of Advanced Studies) in Mathematical Statistics at the University of Cambridge and the M2A (Master 2 in Algorithms and Learning) at Sorbonne Université. During my time at University of Cambridge, I wrote my Part III Essay on Missing and Unmeasurable Data, under the supervision of Professor John Aston. My studies were brought to completion by undergoing a research internship on Set-Valued Classification under Fairness Constraints, under the supervision of Christophe Denis and Mohamed Hebiri.
